FAQ

Questions People
Always Ask

Is this a generic plan or actually built for me? +

Everything is built specifically for you — your schedule, your goals, your starting point. I don't hand out templates. The whole point is that this fits your life, not the other way around.

What app do you use for coaching? +

Your training plan is delivered through a dedicated coaching app. It tracks your sessions, logs your progress, and keeps everything in one place — simple and easy to follow from day one.

I've tried things before and they haven't worked — why is this different? +

Because this time the plan is built around your actual life, not a one-size-fits-all programme. Most people fail not because of a lack of effort, but because they're following something that was never designed for them.

What if I don't have much time to train? +

That's exactly what the plan accounts for. Whether you've got 3 hours a week or 6, the programme is designed around what you actually have — not what would be ideal in a perfect world.

Can I cancel at any time on the 4-week plan? +

Yes. The 4-week rolling package has no long-term commitment. You stay because it's working — not because you're locked in.

How do I get in touch with you during coaching? +

Directly. No support team, no chatbot. You message me and I respond. That's part of what makes this different from a faceless online programme.
